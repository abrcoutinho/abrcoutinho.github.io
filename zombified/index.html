What is it?

A competitive party game with intuitive tasks that stimulate exercises for the brain.

How it works

  • You should invite friends to play and avoid being zombified by fulfilling tasks;
  • Earn coins for good performance;
  • The 'zombiometer' will be fed from your mistakes.

Where is the wellbeing?

  • The brain needs exercise to stay active;
  • Getting out of the rut and avoiding to do everything in automatic helps to boost memory and concentration;
  • Party games are a great way to keep people in activity together.
