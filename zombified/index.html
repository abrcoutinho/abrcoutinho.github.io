The Hive Brazil design test, april 2018

What is?

A competitive party game with intuitive tasks that stimulate exercises for the brain.

Why neurobics?

  1. The brain needs exercises to stay active;
  2. Getting out of the rut and avoiding everything in automatic helps to boost memory and concentration;
  3. Party games are a great way to keep people in activity together.
